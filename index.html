How it Works

  • Real-time SMS Alerts

    Receive instant notifications on your mobile phone when excess food is available at discounted prices.

  • Discounted Food Listings

    Our mobile app and website display a list of available excess food items, along with their discounted prices.

  • Waste Reduction Analytics

    The system provides cafeteria managers with detailed analytics on food waste reduction, including metrics on total waste reduction, cost savings, and customer engagement.

Key Features

  • Food Item Categorization

    Excess food items are categorized into meal types (breakfast, lunch, dinner) and dietary preferences (vegetarian, non-vegetarian).

  • Reward Points System

    Students earn reward points for purchasing excess food items, which can be redeemed for discounts on future purchases or other campus services.

  • Student-Friendly Interface

    Our mobile app and website are designed to be user-friendly and easy to navigate, making it simple for students to find and purchase excess food items.
