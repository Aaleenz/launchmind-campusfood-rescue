CampusFood Rescue

Reduce food waste, save money, and eat sustainably on campus.

Features

  • Real-time SMS Alerts

    Get notified within 30 seconds when food is available, and save money on meals.

  • Discounted Food Listings

    Enjoy 30-50% discounts on leftover food, and customize your preferences.

  • Waste Reduction Analytics

    Track waste reduction and revenue growth in real-time, and make informed decisions.

How it Works

  1. Get registered as a student or cafeteria manager.
  2. Post leftover food deals or receive SMS alerts when food is available.
  3. Customize your preferences and track waste reduction and revenue growth.
