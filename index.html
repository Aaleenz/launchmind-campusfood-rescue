Eat Well, Save Money, Reduce Waste

CampusFood Rescue helps students save money and reduce food waste by providing real-time notifications about available leftover campus cafeteria food at discounted prices.

How it Works

Real-time Notifications

Students receive instant SMS alerts when food is available at discounted prices, allowing them to plan their meals and save money.

Discounted Prices

Food is offered at significantly reduced prices to encourage students to purchase it and reduce waste.

Food Inventory Management

Cafeteria staff can easily track and manage the inventory of leftover food, making it easier to identify what's available and when.

Secure Payment Processing

Students can easily pay for food using a secure online payment system, making it convenient and hassle-free.

How it Works

  1. Cafeteria staff prepare leftover food for sale at discounted prices.
  2. Students receive real-time notifications about available food through SMS alerts.
  3. Students purchase food at discounted prices using a secure online payment system.
  4. Cafeteria staff manage food inventory and track sales data.
