Our Key Features

Real-time Notifications

Receive instant SMS alerts when leftover food is available at discounted prices

Personalized Offers

CampusFood Rescue uses machine learning algorithms to analyze students' preferences and offer personalized deals

Analytics for Waste Reduction

Cafeteria managers gain access to detailed analytics on food waste, allowing them to identify trends and make data-driven decisions

How it Works

Step 1

Students opt-in to receive real-time notifications about available food

Step 2

CampusFood Rescue sends personalized deals to students based on their preferences

Step 3

Students purchase discounted food using their university ID or mobile wallet
