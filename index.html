CampusFood Rescue

Save money, reduce waste, and eat affordably on campus.

How it works

Real-time SMS Alerts

We use Twilio's SMS gateway to send real-time notifications to students when food is available, with a 30-second delivery SLA.

Discounted Food Listings

We offer 30-50% discounts on leftover food, with pricing strategy based on food type, quantity, and proximity to the student's location.

Waste Reduction Analytics

We track and display real-time waste reduction metrics on our manager dashboard, including a 40% waste reduction goal within 6 months.

Our Process

  1. Cafeteria managers post leftover food deals.
  2. Students receive real-time SMS alerts with food options.
  3. Students purchase food at discounted prices.
