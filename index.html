CampusFood Rescue

Save money on leftover campus cafeteria food while reducing food waste for cafeteria managers.

How it Works

CampusFood Rescue is a real-time notification system that helps students save money on leftover campus cafeteria food while reducing food waste for cafeteria managers.

Real-time Notification System

Students receive SMS alerts about available leftover food at discounted prices, with options to filter by food type, price, and dietary preferences.

Food Tracking and Inventory Management

Cafeteria managers can easily track and manage their food inventory, including the types of food available, quantities, and expiration dates.

Waste Reduction Analytics

Cafeteria managers receive detailed analytics on food waste reduction, including metrics on the amount of food rescued, waste reduction rates, and cost savings.
