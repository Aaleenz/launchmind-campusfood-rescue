Reduce Food Waste. Save Money.

CampusFood Rescue: A real-time notification system connecting students with discounted leftover cafeteria food.

Our Key Features

  • Real-time Notifications

    Students receive push notifications and SMS alerts when discounted leftover food is available.

  • Discounted Prices

    Students can purchase leftover food at discounted prices, saving them money and reducing food waste.

  • Food Availability Dashboard

    Cafeteria managers can view a dashboard showing the types and quantities of leftover food available.

How it Works

  1. Cafeteria managers list leftover food on the platform.
  2. Students receive real-time notifications about available food.
  3. Students purchase leftover food at discounted prices.

Stay up-to-date with the latest deals and discounts from your campus cafeteria.
