CampusFood Rescue

Save money, reduce food waste

How it works

  • Real-time Notifications

    Receive SMS alerts when food is available at discounted prices

  • Food Availability Dashboard

    Cafeteria managers view available food items, quantities, and prices in real-time

  • Ordering and Payment System

    Students place orders and make payments through the CampusFood Rescue app

Our Process

  1. Cafeteria managers list available food items and prices
  2. Students receive real-time notifications about available food items
  3. Students place orders and make payments through the CampusFood Rescue app
