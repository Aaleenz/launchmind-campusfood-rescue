CampusFood Rescue

Save Money, Eat Well, Reduce Waste

Key Features

Real-time Food Availability Notifications

Students receive SMS alerts when food is available at discounted prices, and cafeteria managers can schedule notifications in advance.

Discounted Prices for Leftover Food

Students can purchase leftover food at discounted prices, and cafeteria managers can set custom prices for different types of food.

Food Waste Analytics

Administrators can track and analyze food waste data to identify trends and areas for improvement, and cafeteria managers can use this data to inform their food planning and purchasing decisions.

How it Works

  1. Cafeteria managers schedule notifications for available food.
  2. Students receive SMS alerts and can purchase leftover food at discounted prices.
  3. Administrators track and analyze food waste data to identify areas for improvement.
