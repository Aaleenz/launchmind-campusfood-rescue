CampusFood Rescue

Save money, reduce waste, and enjoy your favorite campus meals

How We Help

Real-time Notifications

Receive SMS alerts when food is available at discounted prices in the cafeteria.

Food Availability

View a list of available food items, including prices and quantities, so you can make informed decisions.

Analytics and Reporting

Cafeteria managers receive detailed reports on food waste reduction and student engagement.

How it Works

  1. Download our app or visit our website
  2. Search for food in your campus cafeteria
  3. Receive real-time notifications about available food
  4. Enjoy your meal and reduce waste!
