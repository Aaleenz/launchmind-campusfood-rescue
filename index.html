How it Works

Here's how CampusFood Rescue connects students with discounted leftover campus food:

  1. Cafeteria managers post leftover food deals in real-time
  2. Students receive SMS alerts when discounted food becomes available
  3. Students purchase discounted food using their campus ID or credit card
