How it Works

CampusFood Rescue is a platform that helps students save money by reducing food waste and providing affordable meals. Here's how it works:

  1. Students receive real-time notifications about leftover food availability.
  2. Students can browse available food options and place orders through our mobile app.
  3. Cafeteria managers receive analytics and insights on food waste to optimize their operations.
