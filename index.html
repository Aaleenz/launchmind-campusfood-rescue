CampusFood Rescue

Reduce food waste, save money, eat well

Our Key Features

Real-time Food Availability Notifications

Students receive SMS alerts when leftover food is available at discounted prices.

Personalized Food Recommendations

The app suggests food options based on students' dietary preferences and allergies.

Food Waste Reduction Analytics

Cafeteria managers gain insights into food waste patterns and receive recommendations.

How it works

  1. Students sign up for SMS alerts or email notifications.
  2. Cafeteria managers post available food options at discounted prices.
  3. Students receive notifications and purchase food through the app.
