CampusFood Rescue

Save money, reduce waste, and eat smart

Our Key Features

Real-time Food Alerts

Students receive push notifications when leftover food is available at discounted prices in the cafeteria.

Personalized Food Recommendations

The system suggests food options based on students' dietary preferences and allergies.

Food Waste Analytics

Cafeteria managers receive detailed reports on food waste reduction and can track the effectiveness of the system.

How it Works

  1. Students sign up for CampusFood Rescue
  2. Students receive real-time notifications of discounted leftover meals
  3. Students pay for meals using a mobile app or online platform
  4. Cafeteria managers track food waste and optimize their operations
