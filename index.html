How we work

  • Real-time SMS Alerts

    Receive notifications within 30 seconds when discounted meals are available near you

  • Discounted Food Listings

    Get 30-50% off leftover food with pricing strategies like 'Buy One Get One Free' and '50% Off'

  • Waste Reduction Analytics

    Track and display waste reduction metrics and revenue reports for cafeteria managers

How it works

Here's how our system works:

  1. Cafeteria managers post leftover food deals on our platform
  2. Students receive real-time SMS alerts about discounted meals
  3. Students can view discounted food options sorted by distance and filter by food type and price range
  4. Cafeteria managers track waste reduction metrics and revenue reports
