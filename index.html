CampusFood Rescue

Save money, reduce waste, and eat well with our real-time notification system.

How it Works

CampusFood Rescue is a real-time notification system that reduces food waste and saves money for students by offering leftover campus cafeteria food at discounted prices.

  • Real-time Notifications

    Students receive instant SMS alerts when leftover food is available at discounted prices, allowing them to grab a meal at a lower cost.

  • Food Availability Dashboard

    Cafeteria managers can view a real-time dashboard showing available food items, quantities, and prices, making it easier to manage inventory and reduce waste.

  • Analytics and Reporting

    Administrators can access detailed reports on food waste reduction, allowing them to track progress and make data-driven decisions to improve sustainability efforts.
