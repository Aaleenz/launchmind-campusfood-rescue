Get real-time notifications on available discounted food options at your campus cafeteria.

Key Features

  • Real-time SMS Alerts

    Receive instant notifications on available discounted food options through SMS.

  • Discounted Food Listings

    Browse a list of discounted food options, including meal deals, leftovers, and special promotions.

  • Waste Reduction Analytics

    Access data-driven insights on food waste reduction, helping cafeteria managers optimize food production.

How it Works

  1. Cafeteria managers list leftover food and promotions.
  2. Students receive real-time SMS alerts with available deals.
  3. Students browse discounted food options and make informed dining choices.
