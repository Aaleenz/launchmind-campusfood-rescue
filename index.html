CampusFood Rescue

Reduce food waste. Save money. Eat well.

How it works

  • Real-time Notifications

    Students receive immediate notifications when leftover cafeteria food is available for purchase, including the food item, price, and location.

  • Discounted Prices

    Students can purchase leftover cafeteria food at discounted prices, with options for cash, credit, or mobile payment.

  • Waste Reduction Analytics

    Cafeteria managers receive detailed analytics on food waste, including types of food wasted, quantities, and times of day, to inform inventory management and waste reduction strategies.

How it works

  1. Students download the CampusFood Rescue app or sign up for email updates.
  2. Students receive real-time notifications when leftover cafeteria food is available for purchase.
  3. Students purchase leftover cafeteria food at discounted prices.
  4. Cafeteria managers receive detailed analytics on food waste to inform inventory management and waste reduction strategies.
