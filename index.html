CampusFood Rescue

Reduce food waste and save money with our real-time notification system

How We Work

Real-time Notification System

Students receive SMS alerts when food is available at discounted prices, with options to filter by cuisine, dietary restrictions, and time of day.

User-Friendly Interface

Students can easily sign up and manage their accounts, view available food options, and purchase discounted meals through the CampusFood Rescue app or website.

Analytics Dashboard

Cafeteria managers can track waste reduction, sales data, and customer engagement through a customizable dashboard, providing insights to inform menu planning and operations.

How it Works

  1. Download the app or access the website
  2. Sign up for an account and verify your phone number
  3. Receive real-time notifications about available food options
  4. Purchase discounted meals through the app or website

Join the Movement!

Sign up for our email newsletter or SMS alerts to stay up-to-date on the latest CampusFood Rescue news and promotions.
