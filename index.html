CampusFood Rescue

Save money, reduce waste, and eat well with our real-time food notification system

How it Works

Real-time Food Availability Notifications

Students receive SMS alerts when leftover food is available at discounted prices

Personalized Food Recommendations

Students can filter food options based on dietary restrictions, preferences, and allergies

Waste Reduction Analytics

Cafeteria managers can track food waste and identify areas for improvement

How It Works

CampusFood Rescue is a simple and effective solution for reducing food waste and saving money on campus.

  1. Students sign up for our service and receive real-time notifications about available food
  2. Students can filter food options based on their dietary needs and preferences
  3. Cafeteria managers track food waste and identify areas for improvement
