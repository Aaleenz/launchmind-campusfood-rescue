Reduce Food Waste, Save Money

Get real-time notifications about leftover campus cafeteria food at discounted prices.

Our Key Features

  • Real-time Notification System

    Students receive SMS alerts when leftover food is available at discounted prices.

  • Discounted Prices

    Leftover food is sold at significantly reduced prices to incentivize students to purchase and reduce waste.

  • Food Waste Analytics

    Cafeteria managers can track and analyze waste reduction efforts, identifying areas for improvement and optimizing food production.

How it Works

  1. Students sign up for SMS alerts or email notifications.
  2. Cafeteria managers post leftover food availability and prices.
  3. Students receive notifications and purchase leftover food at discounted prices.

Get started today and start saving money while reducing food waste!
